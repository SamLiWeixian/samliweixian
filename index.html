About / Executive Summary

Dr. Sam Li Weixian is a globally recognized AI leader, innovator, and board advisor with 15+ years of experience driving digital transformation across finance, energy, healthcare, and Industry 4.0. As a Chief Data Officer, CTO, and Principal AI Strategist, Dr. Li has led high-impact teams and delivered AI solutions that have improved business outcomes for Fortune 500 companies and millions of users worldwide.

He is the recipient of multiple international awards, including the HPE Asia Pacific Recognition Award, IEEE ITEC Student Travel Scholarship, and Best Dissertation Prize at Newcastle University. Dr. Li holds several US patents, has published 30+ peer-reviewed papers, and is a frequent speaker at global conferences. He is passionate about mentoring, academic supervision, and fostering innovation in both industry and academia.

Dr. Li is also a dedicated volunteer, serving as a career mentor, board advisor, and aikido instructor. He is committed to advancing ethical AI, diversity in tech, and community engagement through various non-profit and educational initiatives.
